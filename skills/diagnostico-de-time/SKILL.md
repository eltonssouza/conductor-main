# Skill — diagnostico_de_time

**When to use:** When there is delivery friction, burnout, or conflict.

**Steps:**
1. Separate the problem into people / delivery / system.
2. Collect signals (DORA metrics, 1:1s, flow data).
3. Identify the root cause and bottleneck.
4. Propose a minimal intervention with an owner and deadline.
5. Define how to measure improvement.
