# Skill — security-program

**When to use:** For strategy and risk at the organizational level.

**Steps:**
1. Inventory assets and map risks.
2. Evaluate against a *framework* (NIST/ISO) and compliance requirements.
3. Prioritize treatment by risk × cost.
4. Define policies, metrics, and incident response.
5. Communicate risk posture to executives.
